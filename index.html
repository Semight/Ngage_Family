Breakfast & After School Club

We are excited to announce the launch of our breakfast and after school club program. Our aim is to provide a safe and fun environment for your children outside of regular school hours. Here are some reasons why you should consider enrolling your child in our program:

  • Engaging and fun activities
  • Flexible timings to accommodate your schedule
