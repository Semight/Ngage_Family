About Us

BUILDING SCHOOLS AND EMPOWERING COMMUNITIES IN AFRICA.

Family Ngage is a community interest company that works to empower individuals and families by improving their quality of life. We offer various services and projects that aim to create positive changes in communities, particularly focusing on disadvantaged and hard-to-reach families.

Our goal is to connect with the less privileged, providing affordable and free services where possible, while reaching out to resources available through government agencies and non-governmental organizations (NGOs).

The rate of teenage pregnancies and single motherhood, especially within minority groups, is alarmingly high and cannot be solved by government agencies alone. Furthermore, the rising rates of youth crime leading to imprisonment, violence, and other antisocial behaviors must not be ignored. Family Ngage is committed to addressing these issues with the support of the community.

Our objectives include providing mentorship, career guidance, employability support, and developing self-employment skills. We organize empowering events, outreach programs, and community integration projects aimed at improving the well-being of families and youths within minority groups.
